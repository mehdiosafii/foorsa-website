China University Admission Requirements for Moroccan Students

Academic Requirements

  • Minimum Bac grade: 10/20 for most universities, 14/20+ for top universities
  • Science stream: Eligible for all programs (Engineering, Medicine, Business, etc.)
  • Economics stream: Business, Economics, Law, International Trade, Management
  • Literature stream: Languages, Arts, Education, Philosophy, Journalism
  • CSCA exam: Required by 306+ universities starting 2026

Language Requirements

  • English-taught programs: IELTS 5.5–6.5 or TOEFL 60–90
  • Chinese-taught programs: HSK 4–5 (1-year Chinese prep available)
  • No certificate: Some universities offer foundation year programs

Costs & Budget

  • Tuition: $2,000 – $8,500/year (varies by university and program)
  • Living costs: $300 – $800/month depending on the city
  • CSC Scholarship: Covers tuition + accommodation + monthly stipend (2,500 CNY)
  • Other scholarships: University, provincial, and Belt & Road scholarships available

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I study in China with my Moroccan Bac?

Yes! Chinese universities accept the Moroccan Baccalaureate for undergraduate admission. Your chances depend on your grade (minimum 10/20 for most universities), your stream, and the university's specific requirements. Use our calculator above to check your eligibility.

What Bac grade do I need for a CSC scholarship?

For CSC Type A (full scholarship), you typically need 14/20 or higher. For CSC Type B (tuition waiver), 12/20 is usually sufficient. University and provincial scholarships may accept lower grades. The stronger your profile (grade + English + motivation), the better your chances.

Is this calculator accurate?

Our calculator provides estimates based on real admission data from 49 Chinese universities. While results are indicative, actual admission depends on many factors including application quality, competition, and specific university policies. Contact Foorsa for a detailed, personalized assessment.

Can I study in China with an Economics or Literature Bac?

Absolutely! Economics Bac holders can study Business, Finance, Law, International Trade, and Management. Literature Bac holders can study Languages, Arts, Journalism, Education, and more. Science Bac gives the widest choice including Engineering and Medicine.

How can Foorsa help me apply to Chinese universities?

Foorsa provides end-to-end support: university selection, document preparation, scholarship applications (CSC, university, provincial), visa processing, CSCA exam prep, and travel arrangements. We've helped hundreds of Moroccan students study in China.
