Pro Tips

Document Preparation Tips

Follow these expert recommendations to ensure a smooth application process.

  • 1

    Get Certified Translations

    Ensure all documents are translated into English or Chinese by a certified translator if they are in another language.

  • 2

    Prepare Proper Photos

    Photos must have a white background and meet passport photo specifications. Prepare multiple copies for different applications.

  • 3

    Start Early

    Start gathering your documents early. Some documents like medical tests and clearance certificates may take weeks to obtain.

  • 4

    Stay Organized

    Keep both digital copies and original documents organized. You may need to submit scans first and present originals later.

  • 5

    Check Validity Dates

    Ensure your passport has at least 18 months validity and all certificates are within their acceptance period.
