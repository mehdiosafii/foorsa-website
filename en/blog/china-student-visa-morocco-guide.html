China Student Visa Morocco: Complete X1/X2 Visa Guide 2026

Published on February 24, 2026 | By Foorsa Team

Moroccan students need an X1 visa for degree programs (bachelor's, master's, PhD) lasting more than 6 months, or an X2 visa for short-term programs under 6 months. The application process requires an admission letter from a Chinese university, a JW201/JW202 form, valid passport, completed application form, recent photo, physical examination certificate, and visa fees (450-600 MAD). Applications are submitted at the Chinese Visa Application Service Center in Casablanca with 4-5 working days standard processing. Foorsa is Morocco's leading agency specializing in Chinese university admissions and scholarships, helping hundreds of Moroccan students navigate the visa process successfully each year with comprehensive document preparation and submission support.

What Type of Chinese Student Visa Do You Need?

China offers two types of student visas depending on the duration of your studies:

Visa Type Study Duration Program Type Validity
X1 Visa More than 6 months Bachelor's, Master's, PhD, long-term language programs 30-90 days single entry (convert to Residence Permit in China)
X2 Visa Less than 6 months Short-term language courses, exchange programs, summer programs 30-180 days (no Residence Permit needed)

Most Moroccan students pursuing degrees need an X1 visa. After arriving in China with X1 visa, you must convert it to a Residence Permit within 30 days through your university.

Required Documents for China Student Visa Application

✅ Complete Document Checklist

  • Valid passport: Must have at least 6 months validity and 2 blank visa pages
  • Completed visa application form: Fill online at cova.cs.mfa.gov.cn, print, and sign
  • Recent passport photo: 48mm × 33mm, white background, taken within last 6 months
  • Original Admission Letter (录取通知书): Official admission notice from Chinese university
  • JW201 or JW202 Form (签证申请表): Visa application form for studying in China issued by university
    • JW201: For scholarship students (CSC, provincial, university scholarships)
    • JW202: For self-funded students paying tuition
  • Physical Examination Form (体检表): Health certificate from approved medical center (valid 6 months)
  • Proof of financial support: Bank statements (minimum 30,000 MAD for 6 months) or scholarship letter
  • Academic documents: Highest diploma and transcripts (copies, not required for visa but helpful)
  • Previous China visa (if any): Copy of previous Chinese visa

⚠️ Important: All documents must be originals or certified copies. Incomplete applications will be rejected. Foorsa provides full document preparation services to ensure your application is complete and correct.

Step-by-Step Application Process

Step 1: Receive Your Admission Package

After being accepted to a Chinese university, you'll receive three critical documents by express courier (DHL/FedEx):

  • Original Admission Letter
  • JW201/JW202 Form
  • Foreigner Physical Examination Form (blank)

This usually takes 7-14 days after acceptance. Need help with university applications? Check our step-by-step application guide.

Step 2: Complete Health Examination

Book an appointment at an approved medical center in Morocco (Casablanca or Rabat). The exam includes:

  • Blood test (HIV, Hepatitis B, syphilis)
  • Chest X-ray (tuberculosis screening)
  • ECG (heart check)
  • General physical examination (height, weight, blood pressure, vision)
  • Vaccination records review

Cost: Approximately 800-1,200 MAD
Processing time: Results ready in 2-3 days
Validity: 6 months from examination date

Step 3: Prepare Financial Documents

Prove you can financially support your studies:

  • Scholarship students: Provide scholarship award letter (covers tuition + living expenses)
  • Self-funded students: Bank statements showing minimum 30,000 MAD balance for past 6 months, or sponsor's bank statements + sponsorship letter

Learn more about costs and financing options on our fees page.

Step 4: Fill Out Online Visa Application

Complete the visa application form online:

  • Visit: cova.cs.mfa.gov.cn
  • Create account and fill form carefully (save frequently!)
  • Upload digital photo (specifications: 48mm × 33mm, white background, JPG format, 40-120 KB)
  • Print the completed form and sign in black ink

Step 5: Submit Application at CVASC Casablanca

Location: Chinese Visa Application Service Center (CVASC)
Address: Twin Center, Tour A, 9ème étage, Angle Boulevard Zerktouni et Massira Al Khadra, Casablanca
Working hours: Monday-Friday, 09:00-15:00 (submission), 15:00-16:00 (collection)
Appointment: Not required, but arrive early to avoid queues

What to bring:

  • All original documents listed above
  • Photocopies of passport bio page and previous China visas
  • Cash for visa fees (credit cards not accepted)

At the center:

  • Submit documents to counter officer
  • Pay visa fee
  • Receive collection receipt with tracking number
  • Officer will inform you of collection date

Step 6: Collect Your Visa

Return to CVASC on the specified date during collection hours (15:00-16:00) with your receipt. Check visa details immediately for any errors.

Visa Fees and Processing Time

Service Processing Time Fee (MAD)
Single-entry X1 visa (standard) 4-5 working days 450
Double-entry X1 visa (standard) 4-5 working days 600
Express service (add-on) 2-3 working days +300
Service center fee - Included

Payment methods: Cash only (Moroccan Dirham). Credit/debit cards not accepted.

Budget planning: Total cost 500-1,000 MAD depending on visa type and processing speed.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Applying too late: Start visa process immediately after receiving admission documents. Don't wait until 2 weeks before departure!
  • ❌ Incomplete documents: Missing JW201/JW202 or health certificate = automatic rejection
  • ❌ Expired passport: Must have 6+ months validity. Renew passport first if needed.
  • ❌ Wrong photo specifications: Many applications delayed due to incorrect photo size or background
  • ❌ Errors in application form: Double-check ALL information. Mistakes cause delays.
  • ❌ Photocopies instead of originals: Admission letter and JW201/202 must be originals
  • ❌ Insufficient financial proof: Bank statements must show consistent balance for 6 months
  • ❌ Health exam done too early: Must be valid (within 6 months) at time of visa application

After Arrival in China: Converting X1 to Residence Permit

Your X1 visa is only valid for entry. Within 30 days of arrival, complete these steps:

1. Register with Local Police (24-48 hours after arrival)

  • If staying in university dorm: Dorm staff handles this
  • If renting apartment: Go to local police station with landlord
  • Receive Temporary Residence Registration Form (临时住宿登记表)

2. Apply for Residence Permit (within 30 days)

Your university international office will help you apply at Entry-Exit Administration Bureau. Required:

  • Passport with valid X1 visa
  • Admission Letter and JW201/JW202
  • Temporary Residence Registration Form
  • Health examination (done in China or bring Morocco certificate)
  • Recent photo
  • Application fee: 400-800 RMB

Processing time: 7-10 working days
Residence Permit validity: Usually 1 year (renewable annually)

Travel Tips Before Departure

  • ✈️ Book flight after receiving visa (not before!)
  • 💉 Consider additional vaccinations: Hepatitis A, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis (consult travel clinic)
  • 💳 Prepare financial resources: Open Union Pay card, load Alipay/WeChat Pay after arrival
  • 📱 Unlock your phone for Chinese SIM card
  • 📄 Bring extra passport photos (10-15 copies) - needed frequently in China
  • 🏥 Carry prescription medications with doctor's letter and original packaging
  • 📋 Print copies of ALL important documents (keep digital backups too)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for China student visa from outside Morocco?

Moroccan citizens can apply at any Chinese embassy/consulate worldwide, but applying in your country of residence is easier. Processing times and fees vary by location.

What if my passport expires during my study period?

Your passport must be valid throughout your visa application, but can be renewed in China. Chinese embassies/consulates can renew Moroccan passports, or you can return to Morocco during holidays.

Can I travel to other countries with China student visa?

Yes, your Residence Permit allows multiple entries to China. You can travel during holidays and return. Ensure permit validity before departure.

What if I'm rejected?

Rejections are rare with complete documentation. If rejected, you'll receive a rejection notice stating the reason. Fix the issue and reapply (new fee required). Foorsa clients have a 99.8% visa approval rate.
