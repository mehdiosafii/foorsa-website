CSC Scholarship 2026: Complete Guide for Moroccan Students

Published on February 22, 2026 | By Foorsa Team

The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) is one of the most prestigious and sought-after scholarships for international students wishing to study in China. For Moroccan students, it represents an incredible opportunity to access world-class education at top Chinese universities—completely funded.

What is the CSC Scholarship?

The China Scholarship Council (CSC) is a non-profit institution affiliated with the Ministry of Education of China. Established in 1996, CSC is responsible for providing financial support to Chinese citizens studying abroad and international students coming to study in China.

The CSC Scholarship covers:

  • Full tuition fees
  • Accommodation (on-campus dormitory)
  • Monthly living stipend (2,500-3,500 RMB depending on degree level)
  • Comprehensive medical insurance
  • One-year Chinese language preparatory course (if required)

Eligibility Requirements for Moroccan Students

To be eligible for the CSC Scholarship 2026, Moroccan applicants must meet the following criteria:

Academic Requirements

  • Bachelor's Degree: High school diploma (Baccalauréat) with good grades
  • Master's Degree: Bachelor's degree with minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0 or equivalent
  • PhD Program: Master's degree with strong academic record and research experience

Age Requirements

  • Bachelor's applicants: Under 25 years old
  • Master's applicants: Under 35 years old
  • PhD applicants: Under 40 years old

Language Requirements

While not mandatory, having an HSK certificate (Chinese proficiency test) or English proficiency test (IELTS/TOEFL) strengthens your application significantly. Many programs are taught in English, especially at the graduate level.

CSC Scholarship 2026 Application Process

Step 1: Choose Your Program and University

Research Chinese universities offering programs in your field. Top universities like Tsinghua, Peking University, Zhejiang University, Fudan University, and Shanghai Jiao Tong University are highly competitive but offer excellent programs. Visit our majors page to explore available fields of study.

Step 2: Contact the University

Reach out to the international admissions office and express your interest. Some universities require pre-admission before applying for the CSC scholarship.

Step 3: Prepare Your Documents

Required documents typically include:

  • Completed CSC application form
  • University application form
  • Passport copy
  • Highest diploma and transcripts (notarized and translated)
  • Study plan or research proposal (800-1000 words)
  • Two recommendation letters from professors or employers
  • Language proficiency certificate (HSK, IELTS, or TOEFL)
  • Physical examination form (done at designated clinics)
  • No criminal record certificate

Need help preparing your documents? Check our documents guide for detailed instructions.

Step 4: Submit Your Application Online

Applications are submitted through the CSC online portal (studyinchina.csc.edu.cn) and the university's application system. Make sure to complete both applications.

CSC Scholarship 2026 Important Deadlines

Application Period: January - April 2026 (varies slightly by university)
Final Deadline: April 30, 2026 (for most universities)
Results Announced: June - July 2026
Semester Starts: September 2026

⏰ Important: Some universities have earlier deadlines (March 31). Always verify the specific deadline with your chosen university.

Tips to Win the CSC Scholarship

1. Start Early

Begin preparing your application at least 6 months in advance. Universities with earlier deadlines fill spots quickly.

2. Write a Strong Study Plan

Your study plan is crucial. Clearly explain why you want to study in China, why this specific program, and how it aligns with your career goals. Be specific and genuine.

3. Get Strong Recommendation Letters

Choose recommenders who know your academic abilities well. Professors who supervised your thesis or major projects make excellent references.

4. Apply to Multiple Universities

You can apply to up to 3 universities. Diversify your choices with one top university, one mid-tier, and one safety option.

5. Highlight Your Achievements

Include any research papers, academic awards, internships, or volunteer work. These strengthen your profile significantly.

6. Get Professional Help

At Foorsa, we've helped hundreds of Moroccan students win CSC scholarships. Our team reviews your application, provides feedback, and guides you through every step.

Types of CSC Scholarships

  • Type A (Full Scholarship): Covers everything—tuition, accommodation, living stipend, insurance
  • Type B (Partial Scholarship): Covers one or several items (typically tuition)
  • Type C (One-year Preparatory): For students who need Chinese language preparation before their degree program

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Missing the application deadline
  • Submitting incomplete documents
  • Writing a generic study plan that could apply to any university
  • Poor translation of academic documents
  • Not contacting the university in advance
  • Applying only to highly competitive universities without backup options

After Receiving the Scholarship

Once you receive the CSC scholarship offer letter, you'll need to:

  • Accept the offer officially
  • Apply for your X1 visa at the Chinese Embassy in Morocco
  • Complete pre-departure health checks
  • Book your flight to China
  • Prepare for your arrival and registration at the university
