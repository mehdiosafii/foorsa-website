Privacy Policy

At Foorsa Consulting, the privacy and security of your personal data is of the utmost importance. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website www.foorsa.ma.

  1. Information collection

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information
  • Academic information

We may also collect information automatically when you use our site, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browsing data
  • Cookies and similar technologies
  1. Use of information

The information collected is used to:

  • Process and manage your registrations and orders
  • Communicate with you about your file and our services
  • Communicate with you about our marketing offers newsletter …
  • Personalize your experience on our site
  • Improve our services and the content of our site
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  1. Sharing of information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With third-party service providers who help us manage our operations (provided they adhere to strict privacy standards)
  1. Information protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

  1. Your rights

In accordance with Law No. 09-08 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You may request access to your personal data that we hold.
  • Right of rectification: You may request the correction of your inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request the erasure of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to object: You may object to the processing of your personal data for legitimate reasons.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at the following address: contact@foorsa.ma

  1. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

  1. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated revision date.

  1. Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@foorsa.ma
  • Phone: 08085-58910
  • Address: Av. Fal Ould Oumeir, Rabat 10090
