About the CSC Scholarship Assessment

What is the CSC Assessment?

The China Scholarship Council (CSC) manages the Chinese Government Scholarship — the most prestigious scholarship for international students in China. The assessment evaluates your academic readiness across multiple subjects including mathematics, sciences, language proficiency, and general knowledge about Chinese culture and education.

Why Practice with Our Simulator?

Our free simulator mirrors the real assessment format with timed questions, multiple subjects, and realistic difficulty levels. Practicing regularly helps you identify weak areas, build confidence, and improve your score. Thousands of Moroccan students have used this tool to prepare successfully.

Exam Format

  • 50 multiple-choice questions across 5 subjects
  • 90 minutes total exam time
  • 60% passing score required
  • Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, English, General Knowledge
  • Questions are shuffled randomly each attempt
