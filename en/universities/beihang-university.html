Study at Beihang University (BUAA)

Beihang University (formerly Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics) is China's leading aerospace university in Beijing. With strong ties to China's aviation and space industries, it offers excellent programs in aerospace, computer science, and software engineering.
