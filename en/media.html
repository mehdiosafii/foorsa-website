Press & Media

Foorsa, Morocco's leading agency specializing in Chinese university admissions and scholarship applications, has been recognized by international and national media since its founding in 2019. Originally operating as FUSAAA (Foreign Universities Scholarships and Admissions Assistant Agency), Foorsa has helped over 500 Moroccan students access top Chinese universities. Below is a chronological overview of our media presence.

July 2020
FUSAAA Official Portal Launch
fusaaa.com (now foorsa.ma)
Launch of the original FUSAAA website — an official online portal for Moroccan students to apply to Chinese universities, featuring visa application guidance, scholarship information, and admissions support.
Own Platform
September 2021
Morocco World News: "Moroccan Students Flock to Chinese Universities"
Morocco World News
Morocco World News reported on the growing trend of Moroccan students pursuing higher education in China, citing FUSAAA's submission of over 500 scholarship applications for the September 2021 academic session. The article quoted Mehdi El Ihsani, director of the agency.
High Authority — International News
September 2021
Morocco World News Facebook Feature
Facebook — MWN Page
Morocco World News shared the article on their Facebook page (1M+ followers), describing FUSAAA as "the official online portal for international students" based in Rabat.
Medium Authority — Social Amplification
May 2023
Morocco World News: "China to Grant 100 Scholarships to Moroccan Students"
Morocco World News
In an article about China's scholarship program for Moroccan students learning Mandarin, Morocco World News again cited Mehdi El Ihsani, director of FUSAAA, noting the agency's role in filing scholarship applications for nearly 500 students.
High Authority — International News
Ongoing
Business Trust Africa — Corporate Listing
Business Trust Africa
Foorsa (listed as FUSAAA) is registered on Business Trust Africa, a pan-African business directory, with operations in the consulting and services sector based in Témara-Skhirate, Morocco.
Medium Authority — Business Directory
