Scholarships in China for Moroccan Students

Morocco is a Belt and Road Initiative partner, giving Moroccan students access to exclusive scholarship programs in China. The most popular options include the CSC (Chinese Government Scholarship), the CSC Bilateral Morocco-China program, Belt and Road Scholarship, and provincial government scholarships in Beijing, Shanghai, and Zhejiang.

How to Study in China for Free

Fully funded scholarships cover tuition fees, accommodation, monthly living stipend ($400–$500/month), and health insurance. Many also include a one-time settlement allowance. Use the filters above to find scholarships matching your profile — or apply through Foorsa for personalized guidance.

English-Taught Programs with Scholarships

You don't need to speak Chinese to study in China. Many scholarships support English-taught programs in engineering, medicine, business, computer science, and more. Filter by "English Programs" above to see your options.
