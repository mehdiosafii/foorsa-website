# Competitor SEO Analysis: greatwall.ma vs foorsa.ma
**Date:** 2026-03-07

## Executive Summary
Great Wall has a massive content advantage over Foorsa (62+ blog posts vs 0), but Foorsa's technical SEO foundation is stronger (proper hreflang, Twitter cards, faster page speed). The primary gap is content volume and content strategy.

---

## 1. Content Volume Comparison

| Metric | greatwall.ma | foorsa.ma |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Sitemap structure | 7 sitemaps (posts, pages, categories, tags, videos, geo) | 1 sitemap (~556 URLs across 3 languages) |
| Blog posts | 62+ articles | 0 (listing pages only, no articles) |
| Category pages | 107 URLs across 3 languages | None |
| University pages | ~20 universities | Similar coverage |
| Video sitemap | Yes (dedicated) | Referenced in robots.txt but unconfirmed |
| Geo sitemap | Yes | None |

**Great Wall's content machine targets long-tail keywords** across every stage of the student journey: discovery, planning, cultural adaptation, career.

## 2. Technical SEO Comparison

| Feature | greatwall.ma | foorsa.ma | Winner |

## 3. Content Strategy Analysis

### Great Wall's keyword categories:
- **Discovery:** "study in china", "china universities", "china scholarships"
- **Planning:** "required documents", "degree recognition", "parents guide"
- **Cultural:** "being a muslim in china", "ramadan in china", "living in china"
- **Career:** "work in china", "job opportunities"
- **Per-university:** Bachelor/master subcategories per institution

### Foorsa's content:
- Static service pages (about, contact, FAQ, universities)
- No blog content
- No topical authority building

## Priority Recommendations

### P0 - Immediate (Highest Impact)
1. **Launch a blog** targeting high-volume keywords in ar/en/fr
2. **Add LocalBusiness schema** with address, phone, hours, geo coordinates
3. **Create category/tag taxonomy** for topical clusters

### P1 - Medium-term
4. Add geo sitemap for local SEO
5. Populate video sitemap
6. Create "parent's guide" content in Arabic
7. Add FAQ structured data (FAQPage schema) to FAQ pages

### P2 - Strategic
8. Consider Arabic as primary language at root domain
9. Create per-university content hubs with blog articles
10. Explicitly allow AI search bots (ChatGPT-User, OAI-SearchBot) in robots.txt
