## 💡 Recommendations

### For Future Images

**Always include alt text when adding new images:**

```html
<!-- ✅ Good -->
<img src="new-university.jpg" alt="Zhejiang University campus - Top-ranked Chinese university for international students" />

<!-- ❌ Bad -->
<img src="new-university.jpg" alt="university" />
```

### Alt Text Template

**Formula:** `[Subject] - [Context/Benefit]`

**Examples:**
- "Peking University library - Modern study facilities for international students"
- "Student orientation Beijing - Welcome event for new Moroccan students"
- "Scholarship application form - Apply for full Chinese government funding"
